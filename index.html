About the Brotherhood

No Quarter is a brotherhood of honorable fighters. We do not grief. We do not cheat. We rally, we improve, and we win with discipline.

Purpose

Protect our own. Punish the wicked. Hold the line when others break.

Culture

Respect first. Skill follows. Trash talk stays classy.

Style

Steel and sorcery. Tactics and teamwork. Mythic energy, modern execution.

Code of Honor

  • We fight with honor. Win clean. Lose with respect.
  • We protect the brotherhood. No harassment. No drama farming.
  • We sharpen each other. Teach. Learn. Improve.
  • We give evil no quarter. Toxicity, cheaters, and bullies get removed.
